Admissions guidance

Apply with a clear plan.

A redesigned admissions page should remove uncertainty: students can understand entry requirements, prepare documents, speak to an advisor, and start their application from one place.

How it works

A simpler journey from interest to enrollment

01

Choose a pathway

Compare UWE Bristol, DLC Sri Lanka, and ACCA options by level, duration, awarding body, and career direction.

Check eligibility

Review entry requirements with admissions before submitting documents, especially for top-up and postgraduate routes.

Entry paths

Common entry requirements by level

Foundation

Ordinary Level within 2 sits, including English/Sinhala/Tamil and Maths.

Higher Diploma

Advanced Level credits, Maths/Bio passes, or Ordinary Level plus a relevant foundation pathway.

Undergraduate

Requirements vary by programme. LLB currently lists 112 UCAS tariff points on the original site.

Postgraduate

A bachelor's degree, equivalent qualification, or case by case consideration for relevant professional experience.

Prepare documents

What students should have ready

  • Academic certificates and transcripts.
  • National identity card or passport details.
  • Proof of English proficiency where applicable.
  • Updated CV for postgraduate or professional applications.
  • Any prior learning, diploma, ACCA, or CIMA evidence for top-up routes.

FAQs

Admissions questions

Can students ask before applying?

Yes. The redesigned journey pushes students to speak with an advisor before they commit to the wrong programme.

Should fees be visible on the site?

This can be handled either way. A good compromise is "Request fee details" so the institute captures the lead while still giving students a clear action.

Can PDF downloads become lead capture?

Yes. Programme brochures can be offered after students share their name, phone number, email, and programme interest.
