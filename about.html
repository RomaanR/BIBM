About Bristol

World-class pathways made accessible in Colombo.

Bristol Institute of Business Management was established in 2018 with the aim of providing world-class tertiary education in Sri Lanka and making it affordable to prospective students.

Institution

A modern learning hub for business, law, technology, teaching, psychology, and finance

BIBM offers postgraduate and undergraduate programmes in Business Administration, Accounting and Finance, Law, Information Technology, Teaching, and Psychology through UWE Bristol and DLC Sri Lanka pathways.

The institute is committed to a vibrant and engaging student experience, supported by academic activities, social activities, stakeholder collaboration, and industry expertise.

Vision

To be the premier higher education provider in Sri Lanka.

Mission

To equip students with knowledge, expertise, and a growth-focused learning environment.
